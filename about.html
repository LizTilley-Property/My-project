About Liz Tilley

Media presenter, property storyteller, trusted Brisbane voice.

Passion. Professionalism. Personality.

Liz Tilley is a dynamic real estate media presenter based in Brisbane, Australia. With a background in journalism and a love for property, Liz brings a unique blend of expertise, energy, and authentic storytelling to every project. Her engaging on-camera presence and attention to detail have helped countless properties stand out in a crowded market.

  • Former Sky News and Courier-Mail journalist
  • Hundreds of property videos presented and produced
  • Trusted by leading agents and agencies in Brisbane
  • Known for creative, authentic, and high-converting media
