One-Minute Social Media Reel

Professional voiceover and to-camera presentation, filmed and edited for maximum impact.

What's Included

  • Professional script writing
  • Expert voiceover recording
  • To-camera presentation (PTC)
  • iPhone filming for authentic feel
  • Professional editing and post-production
  • Delivery ready for Instagram, Facebook, TikTok
  • Multiple format versions if needed

Perfect For

Property showcases, brand announcements, product launches, and engaging social media content that needs a professional presenter's touch.

  5. Review and final delivery
